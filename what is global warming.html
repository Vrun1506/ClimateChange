What is Global Warming


The definition of global warming ,according to the Oxford dictionary definition, is a gradual increase in the overall temperature of the earth's atmosphere generally attributed to the greenhouse effect caused by increased levels of carbon dioxide, CFCs, and other pollutants. Global warming has affected everyone in some way or another, be it from the higher risk of natural disasters to the bleaching of the corals in the Great Barrier Reef due to the increase in temperature in seas.

Some of the major effects include:
  • the melting of glaciers
  • the rise in sea levels
  • cloud forests are dying
  • the "lungs" of the world are getting burnt down.

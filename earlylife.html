Greta's early life

Greta Thunberg was born on the 3rd of January 2003. Her mother,Malena Ernman,is a Swedish opera singer and her father,Svante Thunberg,an actor. Thunberg became aware of the threat of global warming from the age of 8, and couldn't understand why so little was being done about it.



From a feeling of helplessness, Thunberg became depressed and often stopped talking- a condition known as selective mutism. She was also diagnosed with Asperger syndrome. Children with Asperger's lack social communication and can end up with repetitive patterns of speech and behaviour. It is often considered a mild form of autism and overlaps with obsessive compulsive disorder or OCD. Thunberg says she doesn't enjoy chitchat and for many years at school was very quiet and shy. But, her personality has been, in many ways a blessing for her role as social activist speaking unpalatable truths.
